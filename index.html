Meeting Schedules

Crimson Defense meets every other Tuesday at 7pm in SERC 2036. As meeting topics become locked-in, they will be posted on Slack and sent to the mailing list.

This semester, you can look forward to learning more about denial of service attacks, malware analysis, case studies, and visit(s) from national cyber security companies!

Resources

  • Join our Slack communication workspace to learn more about the club.

  • Go to our Google Drive to find Kali Linux and Windows XP virtual machines available for download, as well as screen recordings of most of our demo’s.

  • Join our Github to find a comprehensive beginner’s guide. To access the guide, simply scroll down on the main page, past the large logo.

Missions

  • Teach real, applicable security skills (penetration testing, malware analysis, etc.)

  • Advocate for the creation of a Cyber Security Curriculum at the University of Alabama

  • Compete in Cyber Security Competitions at the local, state, and national level
